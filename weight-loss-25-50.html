Lose 25–50 Pounds

Perfect for clients looking to shed moderate weight, reset their metabolism, and build lasting habits. Most clients in this tier see visible results within the first 2–3 weeks.

What's Included

Your dedicated SOTA nutritionist designs a program built around your body, your schedule, and your goals. Every aspect of the plan is personalized — from your meals to your coaching cadence.

Personalized Meal Plan

Custom meal plans designed around foods you enjoy. Eat every 3 hours to stabilize blood sugar and keep your metabolism running at peak efficiency.

Weekly Nutritionist Check-Ins

Phone-based coaching with your dedicated nutritionist who monitors progress, answers questions, and keeps you accountable every step of the way.

Blood Sugar Stabilization

The SOTA Method targets visceral fat by keeping blood sugar balanced. When insulin levels normalize, your body switches from fat-storage mode to fat-burning mode.

Grocery Shopping Lists

Weekly shopping lists for Costco, Sprouts, Trader Joe's, and Central Market so you always know exactly what to buy. No guesswork at the store.

Recipe Library Access

Access to 185+ SOTA-approved recipes with step-by-step instructions and downloadable PDFs. Never get bored with your meals.

No Drugs or Injections

100% food-based weight loss. No medications, no injections, no supplements required. Just real food and expert coaching.

What to Expect

1

Week 1–2: Quick Wins

Most clients notice reduced cravings, better energy, and the scale starting to move within the first week. Your body begins switching to fat-burning mode.
